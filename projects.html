ABOUT MATT

Hey! I'm Matt Druce. You might know me from Guitar & Bass Lessons with Matt Druce. Since I was a kid I've thought there were three things in the world that were cooler than anything else - guitar, animals, and video games. I chose my path in life and started learning guitar at around 10 years old and have stuck with it ever since, resulting in an expansive pool of knowledge surrounding the instrument and music as a whole. Sharing the knowledge I have gathered over the years brings me great satisfaction, and I enjoy seeing my students develop their skills. I have a Bachelor of Music degree from UON, and also run a small indie record label.

When it comes to learning music, I believe keeping an open mind is of the utmost importance, and therefore I am familiar with many different styles, artists and techniques. I would say my approach to teaching is enjoyment-oriented, and based heavily on what each student wants to achieve. Learning anything is SO much easier if it is rewarding. Of course I still stress the importance of proper technique and strong fundamentals, but I recognise that each person learns differently and I am excited to support them to do so.

POLICIES

If a student is unable to make it to a lesson

If you are unable to attend your lesson, you must notify me before 9AM on the day of. If I am notified in time, I will be able to reschedule your lesson for a date within the same term. We will do our best to arrange a catch-up lesson before the end of the term. However, if a suitable time cannot be found, the catch-up lesson will be unavailable.. If you miss the lesson and I am not notified before 9am on the day of I will not be able to reschedule your lesson.

If Matt is unable to make it to a lesson

If I am unable to attend due to sickness, I will let you know before 9AM on the day of. You will be able to choose between a rescheduling within the same term or a refund.

Fees are due within seven days of invoice

If your fees haven't been paid and a payment plan hasn't been arranged by your second lesson of the term, your lesson time may be made available for another student to book

FAQs

Q: What do I need to bring to my lesson?

A: At first - just an eagerness to learn and a guitar if you have one.

Q: Can I sit in on my child's lesson?

A: Of course!

Q: I've arrived early, what do I do?

A: There will be a table with some chairs on the verandah at the back of the house, feel free to take a seat and just hang out.

Q: There's an issue with my guitar, can you fix it?

A: Probably! Alongside my tutoring, I also offer a variety of services including restringing and guitar & amp servicing.
